Weekly Draws

  1. Each Participant may submit more than one (1) entry during the Contest Period.
  2. Two (2) winners will be selected every week based on the shortest possible time taken to complete the weekly game.
  3. In the case of a tie, the winners will be picked at random from the respective eligible entries.
  4. Weekly game winners will not be entitled to participate in any of the remaining Weekly Draws.

Grand Draw

  1. One (1) winner will be picked at random from the Selfie entries.
  2. Any entries made after the Participation Period shall be considered invalid and will not be considered.
